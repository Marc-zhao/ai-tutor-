An AI tutor that guides students through Socratic questioning — asking the right questions so they discover answers themselves. Built for teachers who want to see real learning happen.
🧠
Socratic tutoring
The AI never gives direct answers. It asks questions that guide students to discover solutions themselves — building real understanding, not just completing homework.
✏️
Essay & writing feedback
Students receive targeted questions that help them identify weaknesses in their own writing. The AI prompts revision without rewriting for them.
📊
Cited academic data
Every fact comes with a real source. No invented statistics — only verifiable data from governments, journals, and international organisations.
👩‍🏫
Teacher dashboard
See every student's full conversation history. Identify who is struggling, who is trying to cheat, and what concepts need more class time.
🔒
Anti-plagiarism built in
A two-layer system — the AI never gives direct answers, and a detection layer flags students who repeatedly push for them. Teachers are alerted automatically.
🌐
Bilingual — EN & 中文
The full interface switches instantly between English and Simplified Chinese. Students can interact in their preferred language; the AI responds accordingly.
How it works
Four steps to deeper learning
1
Student asks a question
The student selects a module and types their question — just like any chat tool.
2
AI responds with a question
Instead of answering, the AI asks a guiding question based on what the student already knows.
3
Student thinks it through
The back-and-forth continues until the student arrives at the answer themselves.
4
Teacher reviews & analyses
Every conversation is saved. The teacher dashboard shows progress, weak spots, and risk flags.
