How it works
Four steps to deeper learning
1
Student asks a question
Choose a module and type or upload your question.
2
AI responds with a question
Instead of answering, the AI asks a guiding question.
3
Student thinks it through
The dialogue continues until the student finds the answer.
4
Teacher reviews
All conversations are saved and analysed in the teacher dashboard.
